Download Files for Verification

When you're ready to verify receipts:

Download Spreadsheet
  1. Open the spreadsheet
  2. Click File → Download
  3. Choose Excel (.xlsx) or CSV
Get GCash PDF Statement
  1. Open GCash app
  2. Go to Profile → My Transactions
  3. Generate Statement
  4. Download the PDF
💡 Tip: Save both files somewhere easy to find on your computer.
Open Drive Folder
↓
4

Verify Receipts with the Tool

Use our verification tool to check if the receipts are valid:

  1. Go to the verification website
  2. Upload your spreadsheet
  3. Upload your GCash PDF
  4. Click "Verify Receipts"
  5. View and download results
What the Results Mean:
  • Verified - Receipt matches a real transaction ✓
  • Not Found - No matching transaction found ✗
  • Amount Mismatch - Reference number matches but amount is different ⚠️
  • Duplicate - Same receipt submitted multiple times ℹ️
